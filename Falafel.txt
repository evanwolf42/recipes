Falafel

INGREDIENTS
    2 lbs chickpeas
    2 large onions
    3/4 cup cilantro
    1/2 pinch dill (fresh)
    15 gloves garlic
    3/4 cups parsley leaves
    1 tbsp coriander
    2 tbsp cumin
    1 tbsp salt
    3 pinch ground cardamon
    1 tbsp turmeric
     
    Oil for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Food process everything but the chickpeas
    2. Food process the chickpeas
    3. Mix the two mixtures and re-process
    4. Heat oil to 350 degrees
    5. In small batches fry balls of falafel for approximently 3-5 minutes or until brown
