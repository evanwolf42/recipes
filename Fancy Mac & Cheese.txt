Fancy Mac & Cheese

INGREDIENTS
    1 pound of cooked pasta, smaller noodles desired
    6 slices of bacon, chopped
    1 shallot, diced
    2 garlic cloves, minced
    1/2 pound of brussels sprouts, stems removed and sliced/chopped
    2 tablespoons unsalted butter
    2 tablespoons flour
    2 cups whole milk
    1/2 cup half and half
    8 ounces fontina cheese, freshly grated
    4 ounces white cheddar cheese, freshly grated
    2 ounces parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
    1/4 cup bread crumbs
    2 tablespoons fine breadcrumbs

  Optional:
    6 Strips Bacon
    1-2lbs Ground Beef
    Mushrooms
    Eggs (Crack on top)
    Cheeses
    
    Oregano
    Parsley 
    Rosemary
    Garlic Powder

   After Cooking:
    Avocado
    Parsley 

INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Pre-heat cast iron pan over high heat
    2. Marinate shrimp in bowl
    3. Cook shrimp for ~2 minutes once pan is hot
