French Fries

Ingredients:
    Potatoes
    Salt
    Vinegar

  Toppings:
    Parsley 
    Garlic
    Vinegar

Steps:
    1. Skin and cut up the potatoes, keep coated in water to avoid browning
    2. Boil potatoes in 3 tbsp of salt and 3 tbsp vinegar (~10 min) or until tender.
    3. Leave potatoes to dry and blow off steam.
    4. Fry for about 6 minutes
    5. Put in ziploc bag once cool, squeeze out the air and freeze
    6. Place frozen fries in oil and fry for 6 minutes
