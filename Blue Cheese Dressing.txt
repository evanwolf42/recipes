Blue Cheese Dressing

INGREDIENTS
    1/4 cup mayo
    1/4 cup whole milk
    1/4 cup sour cream
    Dash of Worcestershire sauce
    1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles
    Salt and Pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
    1. To a bowl add the mayo, milk, sour cream and Worcestershire, and stir until combined. Stir in the blue cheese and add salt and pepper to taste.
